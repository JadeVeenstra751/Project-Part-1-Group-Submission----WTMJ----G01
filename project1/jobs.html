Software Developer

Reference Number: SF71T

Description:

Join the innovative team at Leaf Byte Tech as a Software Developer! In this role, you’ll design, build, and maintain cutting-edge software solutions that drive our tech-forward vision. You'll collaborate with cross-functional teams to develop scalable applications, debug complex issues, and continuously improve user experiences. If you’re passionate about clean code, agile development, and making a real impact through technology, we’d love to hear from you!

Responsibilites of this position include:

  4. Identify, troubleshoot, and fix bugs or performance issues.
